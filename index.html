About McGuire’s Lawn & Landscaping

At McGuire’s Lawn & Landscaping, our mission is simple: to create and maintain beautiful, healthy outdoor spaces that our clients can enjoy year-round. We take pride in delivering reliable lawn care and landscaping services built on trust, quality, and attention to detail.

Founded by Chance McGuire, the company has grown from a small start into a trusted local service provider in Southwest Virginia. With every project, we focus on treating each yard like our own—ensuring it looks its best and thrives through every season.

We believe a well-kept lawn and thoughtfully designed landscape add value not only to your property, but also to your everyday life.
